How do I know if online ordering is secure?

Your security is our priority. We use secure bank transfer and UPI (QR code) payments through trusted partners, eliminating the need for storing credit card information. Additionally, our website utilizes SSL encryption and undergoes regular security audits to ensure your transactions remain safe.
